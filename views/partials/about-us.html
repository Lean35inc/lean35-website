Light Weight software development is a translation of lean IT principles and practices to the software development domain. Adapted from the Toyota Production System, a pro-lean subculture is emerging from within the Agile community.

Design UI First

UI First development mandates that the UI component of a system be developed with all of the behavior aspects prior to the development of the business layers. This approach significantly reduces the impact of requirements flux during system implementation.
